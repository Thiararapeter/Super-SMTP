# Super SMTP Plugin Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

At Super SMTP Plugin, we take the security of our software seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

## How to Report a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please contact us directly by emailing [security@thiarara.co.ke](mailto:security@thiarara.co.ke). Provide a detailed description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.

## Our Commitment

Once a vulnerability report is received, we will:

- Acknowledge the receipt of your report in a timely manner.
- Investigate the issue and determine its impact.
- Develop and release patches or updates to fix the vulnerability.
- Communicate with you throughout the process to keep you informed of our progress.

## Responsible Disclosure

We kindly request that you follow responsible disclosure practices and refrain from sharing or publicizing the details of the vulnerability until we have had sufficient time to address it.

## Bug Bounty Program

As a token of appreciation for your responsible disclosure, we may offer a bug bounty or a mention in our release notes. The amount or recognition will be determined based on the severity and impact of the reported vulnerability.

Thank you for your commitment to security and for helping us keep Super SMTP Plugin safe.
